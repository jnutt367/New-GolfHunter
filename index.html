Who Are We?

At GolfHunter LLC, we are revolutionizing the way the game of golf is played.

From the tee to the pin. This ball will always be present and keep you from wondering. The average golfer will play 8-12 rounds of golf per year. That same golfer will lose an average of 2 balls per round, thus losing an average of 36 balls per year and you ask why?

It's quite simple, they can't find them.

Take that number and multiply that by the number of golfers in the US(34.2 million ages 6+), and in one year you are talking 68.4 million golf balls lost in a single year, 300 million lost worldwide.

What Have We Done?

Our revolutionary technology, along with a GPS microchip strategically placed inside the ball, without affecting its integrity, will allow you to track and receive data as to pinpoint its location and how far you have left to the pin.

This technology will keep the microchip powered so that it can relay real time data back to the phone app.
